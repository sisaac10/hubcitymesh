Hub City Mesh


A community owned and run internet serving New Brunswick's youth.

Who We Are


Far too many people do not have access to the Internet. Hub City Mesh is a diverse group of volunteers dedicated to ending the digital divide and extending high-speed, reliable internet to New Brunswick. Hub City Mesh is a neutral network and we do not monitor, collect, store, or block any user data or content.

By joining our network, you agree to extend it to others! You can help by joining our community network to get online, volunteering to connect neighbors or grow the network, and donating to improve our community infrastructure and ensure access for all.

Equipment and Coverage


Our objectives are to:
  • Build a wireless community network backbone to create a long-term solution to the Digital Divide
  • Provide the 5,000+ public school students with free connectivity
  • Expand the network to all city residents for a free or affordable option for fast, reliable internet
